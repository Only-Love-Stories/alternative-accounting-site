Our Services

A complete practice, not a narrow one.

From everyday accounting and tax to SMSF and business advisory — the services a small business, company, trust or individual actually needs, in one place.

01

Accounting & Tax

Small businesses, companies, trusts & individuals

The core compliance work that keeps an organisation in good standing — prepared accurately, lodged on time, and explained in plain terms. We stay across every regulatory and legislative change so you don't have to.

What's included
  • Financial reports & statements
  • Income tax returns — individuals, companies, trusts
  • Business Activity Statements (BAS)
  • Fringe Benefits Tax (FBT) returns
  • Land tax & payroll tax returns
  • Liaison with the ATO & other authorities
02

Xero & Bookkeeping

Businesses wanting clear numbers year-round

Good decisions start with current, accurate books. We set up and tailor Xero to your business, train your team to use it well, and can take on the bookkeeping itself — so your financial picture is never more than a glance away.

What's included
  • Xero setup, migration & tailoring
  • Xero training for your team
  • Monthly management reports
  • Payroll & superannuation
  • Accounts payable & paperless payables
  • Accounts receivable & invoicing
03

SMSF Establishment & Compliance

Individuals & families with a self-managed super fund

Self-managed super gives you control — and a set of obligations to match. We help you establish a fund correctly from the outset, and handle the annual compliance, reporting and lodgement that keep it on the right side of the rules. The independent audit each fund requires is arranged with a registered SMSF auditor, keeping that review properly at arm's length.

What's included
  • SMSF establishment & structuring
  • Trust deed & fund registration
  • Annual financial statements
  • Member reporting & balances
  • SMSF annual return & lodgement
  • Coordination of the independent audit
04

Business Advisory

Owners who want guidance, not just compliance

Beyond keeping you compliant, we help you make better-informed decisions — on how your business is structured, how it's taxed, and where it's heading. Considered advice, grounded in a real understanding of your numbers.

What's included
  • Business structure & entity advice
  • Tax planning & strategy
  • Cash flow & budgeting
  • ASIC & corporate compliance
  • Division 7A & company loan compliance
  • Financial guidance for growth
